Registering a product or service

There are four stages to complete in order to successfully register your product.

  1. Submit your service or product details

    Start

    Provide us details about your service or product.

    Tell us your named contact, security officer and basic product listing details.

  2. Choose your capabilities

    Select the capabilities which your product or service can demonstrate. Capabilities selected will be assesed against these capabilities.

    You will need to provide evidence for your capabilities.

    Learn more about capabilities

    Capabilities are core pieces of functionality that you tell a buyer your product or service can provide.

    In order to successfully register, you will need to demonstrate that you can provide the capabilities you select.

  3. Provide evidence for your standards assesment

    Provide evidence for how your product or service can meet the NHS Digital marketplace standards.

  4. Create a product listing page

    Add information to help buyers evaluate your product or service.

Make your product a success

To successfully set up a product or service you'll need to demonstrate how to fit certain capabilities.
