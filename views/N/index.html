Design hypotheses

Confirm your details interstitial

In user labs and UserZoom testing, users were left confused at what they were being asked of at the "confirm your identity" page.

They didn't understand the following steps of the service nor why they were being asked to submit personal identifiable data.

If we provide an easy to read summary before the "your details" form
Then users will know what they will need to do to authenticate themselves for the service
Because the stages will be shown clearly to them before they start

If we provide an easy to read summary before the "your details" form
Then users will better understand why they are being asked for personal data
Because it will be clearer why that data is being used and what role it plays in verifying the user
