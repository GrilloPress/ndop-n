Register your preference for sharing health data

Register your preference

Use this service to manage your preferences for sharing your patient health information.

You must have an email address or phone number registered with an NHS service to continue online. Other ways to set your preferences instead.

Before you start

To continue you will need:

  • access to your email or mobile phone
  • your NHS number
Where can I find my NHS number?

You can find your NHS number on any document sent to you by the NHS. This may include:

  • prescriptions
  • test results
  • hospital referral letters
  • appointment letters
  • your NHS medical card

Ask your GP for help if you can't find your NHS number
